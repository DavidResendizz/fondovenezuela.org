Fondo Venezuela

Generosity around the World
and Technologies, for help.


Fondo Venezuela (or Venezuela Fund in English) is a mixed fund of associated communities, non-profit organizations, individuals and companies identified with freedom values, concerned about the very particular socio-economic situation of the Venezuelan people. We rely on the generosity around the world and technologies to help.

It is impossible to stay inert before tyranny, crisis and need. Venezuela is a country abducted, plundered and destroyed. With Save The Children, Fondo Venezuela aims to help thousands of children on the border with Colombia, who had fled from their country, and with Bitcoin Venezuela we will help several thousand more within the nation that continues resisting. We not only want to provide relief but to give hope, let them know they are not alone.
